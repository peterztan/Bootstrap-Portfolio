About Me

Hello, my name is Peter Tan. I am currently a Healthcare IT Specialist working at a pain clinic. As a technology enthusiast, I have always enjoyed working with technology, which has inspired me to make the switch into the IT field after working as a Forensic Scientist for two and a half years.

From hardware troubleshooting at a very young age to tapping into video game programming in my free time, the majority of my life has revolved around computers, and therefore coding came naturally for me.

I was originally from China, and I came to the United States when I was but 14 years old. The language barrier proved to be extremely challenging to overcome, but I was able to power through the obstacles and became fluent in English.

My dream is to contribute in making Full Dive Virtual Reality an applicable commercial product instead of a laboratory dream project, help scaling it into something that the everyday consumer can utilize to enrich their lives, enable the disabled and expand the imagination of people everywhere. Additionally, I want to help take Augmented Reality to a new height, in order to increase everyday productivity and work efficiency by providing innovative, interactive and intuitive AR tools.
